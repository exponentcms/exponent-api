exdoc



Finds all of the dates that fall within the monthly recurrence criteria  and within the $start to $end timestamp range.  Monthly recurrence can be  done on a specific date (the 14th of the month) or on a specific weekday / offset  pair (the third sunday of the month).

				timestamp

				 The start of the recurrence range			

					

				timestamp  
				
				$end: 
				
				 The end of the recurrence range			

					

				integer  
				
				$freq: 
				
				 Monthly frequency - 1 means every month, 2 means every    other month, etc.			

					

				bool  
				
				$by_day: 
				
				 Whether or not to recur by the weekday and week offset  (in case of true), or by the date (in case of false).
